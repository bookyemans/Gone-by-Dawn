The Story

She Survived the Bear Once.
Will She Survive Again?

A missing girl. A serial killer's signature. A mother's worst nightmare.

Two days ago, FBI Agent Val Costa came face to face with a serial killer known as the Bear. One of only two women ever to survive, she's forever branded with his signature: a curling "S" carved into her skin. Val knows it's only a matter of time before he takes another victim.

Forced home to recover, Val returns to Red Rose County, California, to be with her son. But the very next day she finds the close-knit community panic-stricken over the disappearance of blonde-haired, freckled-faced local teen, Scarlett Douglas.

The sight of Scarlett's unworn prom dress hanging in her room brings a tear to Val's eye. She won't sleep until this sweet girl is home and safe.

Defying orders, Val interviews the Douglas family at their farmhouse and uncovers a less-than-idyllic picture. Housebound since a devastating diagnosis, Scarlett's world was crumbling.

Val and her team search the nearby towering redwoods for any clue to what happened the morning Scarlett disappeared. But the case takes a sinister turn when a little pink teddy bear is delivered to Val's door.

Did the Bear follow her to Red Rose County? Is her family now in terrible danger? Could Scarlett be his latest victim?

With time running out to save Scarlett's life, and the truth just within her reach, will Val once again come face to face with the Bear? And who ‚Äî if anyone ‚Äî will survive?
